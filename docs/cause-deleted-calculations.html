3.1 Cause-deleted calculations

What if everyone in the population never smoked? This scenario is a cause-deleted scenario.

(ref:causedeleted)

The scenario compares the population with the current exposure status of smoking status and pack-years of smoking to a counter-factual population where these two variables are: smoking status = never smoker, and pack-years of smoking = 0.

The health outcome is calculated for both the current population and it’s counter-factual population, and then compared to each other. The comparison between these two populations show the difference in the health outcome due to the exposure.

There are different cause-deleted calculation you can run:

Original_outcome Counterfactual_outcome Difference_in_outcome
